About SPY

Young people rooted in Christ and community.

SPY is the youth ministry of St. Sebastian Church. We help youth grow closer to God, build strong friendships, serve the parish, and become confident witnesses of faith.

Whether someone is new to church life or already active in ministry, SPY gives them a place to belong, ask questions, pray together, and grow with other young people.

Our Focus Faith. Friendship. Service.

We want every young person to feel known, supported, and encouraged.
